A little about me

I am a creative developer!

I've made my home base in New York City, with my techy hubby. My journey into tech started with a degree in Multimedia in California University State, Northridge.

As I started my first coding class as a requirement for my degree, I was fascinated with how easily I can create an entire universe out of nothing. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own projects to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website for that class, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work.

Now, after graduating in Film, TV & Electronic Media Production, I've added to my multimedia skills web development, where I can use my creative skills and eye for detail to write elegant code and project your attractive ideas into tangible websites.

Can't wait to work with you!
